Platform Features

Everything your agency needs, built into one platform

CDM is a fully integrated system — not a collection of disconnected tools. Every module shares the same data, so nothing falls through the cracks.

👤

Client Management

Full intake and case management — applicant and co-applicant demographics, dependents, income, household composition, HUD-required data fields, activity logs, and follow-up tracking all in one place.

🏠

Property Management

Track property records, photo documentation, lead hazard assessments, insurance, and occupant history. Properties link seamlessly to loans, work plans, and tenants.

📑

Loan & Grant Management

Full loan lifecycle tracking for amortizing installment loans, deferred loans, and grants. Principal, interest, and fees are tracked separately at the loan, project, and program levels — giving you a complete, auditable financial picture at every stage.

🔨

Work & Construction

End-to-end construction workflow — work plans, contractor bidding, bid comparisons, change orders, and disbursements all in one place. Build work plans faster using your own library of reusable work item templates and specifications, and generate job cost estimates directly from those specs before a contractor ever sets foot on site.

💰

Program & Project Management

Track HUD programs (CDBG, HOME, SHIP, and more), community projects, fund drawdowns, and project income. Manage sub-recipients and monitor obligations across all active funding sources.

🏦

Escrow Management

Dedicated escrow module with escrow accounts, fund tracking, annual escrow years, good-standing status, and their own ledger entries — keeping insurance and tax obligations clearly separated from loan ledgers.

⚙️

Automated Billing & Finance

CDM automatically calculates interest, applies fees, generates billing statements, and processes scheduled loan payments every day — so your loan portfolio stays accurate and up to date without your staff having to do a thing.

📊

Built-In Reports

Generate professional PDF and print-ready reports on demand — client demographics, loan portfolios, payment histories, funding draw summaries, contractor activity, work plan progress, and program performance. CDM's reports are designed to make completing your HUD program reporting requirements significantly faster and easier.

🔐

Security & Access Control

Every agency's data is completely private and separate. You decide exactly what each staff member can see and do — from read-only access to full administrative control. Secure login, user management, and a full activity audit trail are all built in.

Data Security

Your agency's data is protected at every layer

CDM handles sensitive resident data, financial records, and HUD-required information. We take that responsibility seriously — security is built into the platform, not bolted on.

🏗️

Hosted on Amazon Web Services

CDM runs on AWS infrastructure — the same platform used by federal agencies and financial institutions. AWS provides physical security, redundant data centers, and 99.9%+ uptime reliability.

🔐

Secure User Authentication

CDM uses enterprise-grade login technology with optional two-factor authentication. The system automatically blocks repeated failed login attempts and protects against unauthorized access — so only your approved staff can get in.

🧱

Account-Level Data Isolation

CDM is a multi-tenant system where every agency's data is fully isolated. One agency can never access another's records — isolation is enforced at the database level on every query.

👥

Role-Based Access Controls

Granular, role-based permissions let you control exactly what each staff member can see and do. Assign roles at the account level, restrict access to sensitive financial data, and audit who did what.

🔒

Encrypted in Transit & at Rest

All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS. Data stored in AWS — including documents, reports, and resident records — is encrypted at rest using AWS-managed encryption keys.

📋

Audit Logging

Critical actions — logins, data changes, document access, and administrative operations — are written to an audit trail. Your agency has a complete, tamper-evident record for compliance and oversight.
